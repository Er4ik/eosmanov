shopfloor.io connects and visualizes your machinery, products and employees; either as the foundation of a scalable and secure Industry 4.0 system architecture or by connecting to your existing infrastructure.

MY ROLE

This is the first big commercial project that started my journey as a full-time web developer. I was involved in product development, implementation of new features, code support and improvement. I also did code reviews and helped my colleagues on a regular basis. After six months of hard work, I was also responsible for onboarding new hires and supporting them through the early stages of the product. Moreover, I often prioritized tasks and distributed them among my colleagues.

The product has a microservice architecture, which was a big challenge for me to improve my programming skills. Also, using modern development tools gave me more energy and excitement for my daily hard work.

PRODUCT

shopfloor.io is an Industrial IoT application for cloud or on-premises operation with which you can connect your systems, products and employees and visualize all relevant data in well-structured cockpits. Tapping into the IoT potential of your machinery creates added value for the manufacturer, the operator and the end-users of the equipment.
